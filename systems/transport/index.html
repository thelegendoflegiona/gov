01 // MANDATE

What the MOT Does.

The Ministry of Transport is the government body responsible for regulating, licensing, and setting policy for all transport operations within and across The LoL's sovereign territory on Skyxion.

DEPT-04 // GOVT OF THE LOL
ALTAËR ERA // 2023–PRESENT
MND-01 // REGULATION

Regulatory Oversight

The MOT sets and enforces standards for all transport operators in The LoL. Licensed operators must comply with national policy, safety requirements, and service-level expectations defined by this Ministry.

MND-02 // LICENSING

Operator Licensing

All transport operators — rail, water, or otherwise — must hold a licence issued by the Ministry of Transport. TL Railways is currently the sole licensed national operator under MOT jurisdiction.

MND-03 // PLANNING

Network Planning

The MOT coordinates national transport planning — approving new routes, endorsing expansion phases, and ensuring transport development aligns with The LoL's wider infrastructure goals.

MND-04 // POLICY

Transport Policy

Responsible for drafting, maintaining, and publishing all national transport policy documents. Policies are deposited in the Legal Archive and remain in force until amended by the Office of the President.

MND-05 // COORDINATION

Cross-Agency Coordination

Works alongside Paiz™ Construction for infrastructure delivery, ISC for security of transport assets, and the Office of the President for megaproject approvals and national strategy.

MND-06 // PUBLIC INTEREST

Citizen Access

The MOT advocates for accessible, reliable transport for all citizens of The LoL. Regular, Express, and Cargo service tiers on TLSRL were defined under this mandate to serve all travel needs.

02 // LICENSED OPERATORS

National Operators.

Transport operators are licensed entities authorised by the Ministry to run services across The LoL's territory. All operations are conducted under a MOT-issued licence.

SUBSIDIARY OF PAIZ® CORP // NATIONAL RAIL OPERATOR
