01 // MANDATE

What the PWD Does.

The Public Works Division commissions, oversees, and maintains all physical infrastructure across The LoL's sovereign territory — from roads and bridges to towers and automated farms.

DEPT-05 // GOVT OF THE LOL
ALTAËR ERA // 2023–PRESENT
MND-01 // CONSTRUCTION

Project Commissioning

The PWD commissions all government-approved construction projects within The LoL. From the initial brief to final handover, every national build passes through this Division.

MND-02 // ROADS

Roads & Pathways

Responsible for planning, building, and maintaining all national roads, pathways, and land routes connecting The LoL's districts, outposts, and points of interest across Skyxion.

MND-03 // STRUCTURES

Public Structures

Overseeing all major built structures — towers, landmarks, civic buildings, and defensive walls — ensuring they meet national standards and are maintained in good condition.

MND-04 // MAINTENANCE

Upkeep & Repairs

The PWD is responsible for the ongoing upkeep of all national infrastructure. This includes post-incident repairs — such as the restoration of TLCC following the 2023 TNT attack.

MND-05 // RESOURCES

Resource Infrastructure

Planning and maintaining national resource facilities — automated farms, storage depots, and production infrastructure — that underpin The LoL's economic and citizen welfare capacity.

MND-06 // COORDINATION

Multi-Agency Delivery

Works alongside the Ministry of Transport on transport infrastructure, ISC on security of public assets, and reports megaproject progress directly to the Office of the President.

National Road Network

The LoL's land-based road and pathway network — connecting districts, outposts, and key landmarks within sovereign territory. TLSRL Railway

The LoL–Spawn Railway Link — built by Paiz™ Construction under PWD oversight and handed over to TL Railways for operation under the Ministry of Transport. The physical infrastructure (bridges, tunnels, stations) remains a PWD asset; the transport service is regulated by MOT.
