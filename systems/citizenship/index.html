01 // OVERVIEW

What is The LoL Citizenship?

Citizenship in The Legend of Legiona is a formal declaration of allegiance and membership within The LoL's sovereign territory on Skyxion. It is not permanent — it is bound to the active server era, and may be granted, renewed, or revoked by the Government at any time.

STATUS-01 // DURATION

Temporary

Citizenship is era-bound. When the Skyxion server resets, all citizenships expire automatically. There is no such thing as permanent The LoL citizenship — every citizen must reapply to maintain their status for each new era.

STATUS-02 // CONTINUITY

Renewable

Past citizens in good standing are eligible for a streamlined renewal upon server reset. Your history with The LoL is considered. Renewals are submitted through the same application portal, with a separate renewal path.

STATUS-03 // AUTHORITY

Revocable

The Office of the President and the Government of The LoL hold the unconditional right to revoke any citizenship at any time, from any location, without prior notice — as defined in THELOLGOV-29-03-2026-0002.

02 // ELIGIBILITY

Who Can Apply?

Any active player on the Skyxion server may apply for citizenship in The LoL. Applications are reviewed against the criteria below. Meeting all requirements does not guarantee citizenship — the Government's decision is final.

Requirements — Must Meet All
  • ✓
    Active Skyxion PlayerYou must be an active player on the Skyxion server during the current era (ALTAËR) at the time of application.
  • ✓
    Age: 16 or Older (IRL)All applicants must be at least sixteen (16) years of age in real life. No exceptions will be made to this requirement.
  • ✓
    Clean Server RecordNo active bans, suspensions, or unresolved violations with Skyxion server administration (Kawaiisho) at the time of application.
  • ✓
    No Outstanding Issues With The LoLNo unresolved disputes, penalties, or prior citizenship revocations from The LoL without a formal pardon.
  • ✓
    Willingness to ComplyFull agreement to all obligations of citizenship as defined in this portal and the Rights Charter (THELOLGOV-29-03-2026-0003).
Disqualifications — Any One Applies
  • ✕
    Active Server BanPlayers currently banned from Skyxion are ineligible until the ban is fully and officially lifted by Kawaiisho administration.
  • ✕
    Prior Revocation Without PardonFormer citizens whose citizenship was revoked by presidential decree are ineligible unless a formal pardon has been issued by the Office of the President.
  • ✕
    Under 16 Years of Age (IRL)Applications from individuals below the age threshold are automatically rejected and will not be reviewed.
  • ✕
    Declared or Known Hostile IntentAny applicant with known hostile intent toward The LoL, its citizens, or allied entities will be denied without review.
  • ✕
    Membership in a Hostile EntityActive membership in a group or nation that is in declared conflict with The LoL may result in automatic disqualification.
03 // PROCESS

How It Works

The application process is direct and fully online. Complete all steps accurately. Incomplete or falsified applications will be immediately rejected.

01
STEP-01 // VERIFY

Check Eligibility

Read all requirements and disqualification criteria carefully. Confirm you are 16+, hold a clean server record, and are prepared to honour every obligation of citizenship.

02
STEP-02 // SUBMIT

Submit Application

Complete the official form. Provide your call name, Minecraft IGN (case-sensitive), and contact details. Choose Apply or Renew as appropriate.

03
STEP-03 // REVIEW

Government Review

The Office of the President reviews all submissions. Server records and eligibility are verified. You will be contacted via the details you provided. This may take some time.

04
STEP-04 // STATUS

Citizenship Granted

Upon approval, you are recognised as an official citizen of The Legend of Legiona for the current Skyxion era. Citizenship must be renewed after each server reset.

04 // OBLIGATIONS

Your Responsibilities

By accepting citizenship, you agree to uphold the following at all times. Failure to comply is grounds for citizenship revocation without notice under THELOLGOV-29-03-2026-0002.

OB-01

Follow Skyxion Server Rules

All citizens must abide by the official rules set by Kawaiisho server administration at all times. Server-wide rules always take precedence.

OB-02

Do Not Cause Chaos

Citizens must not initiate or participate in griefing, sabotage, provocation, or any behaviour that destabilises The LoL or the wider Skyxion community.

OB-03

Respect All Nations & Squads

As a LoL citizen you represent the nation. Treat all other squads, teams, and nations with respect. Do not act with terror or unprovoked hostility toward any party.

OB-04

Tolerate & Include Others

The LoL is built on unity. Citizens must practice mutual tolerance. Targeted harassment, discrimination, or deliberate exclusion of any individual is strictly prohibited.

OB-05

Be Open to Others' Opinions

Citizens must not dismiss or cut off the views of others. Constructive dialogue is central to The LoL's egalitarian governance model. Listen before you speak.

OB-06

Maintain Good Standing

Any new server violations, misconduct, or issues arising after citizenship is granted must be disclosed to the Government. Deliberate concealment is grounds for immediate revocation.

OB-07

Renew After Each Server Reset

Citizenship expires automatically at each server reset. Active citizens in good standing must submit a renewal application to retain their status for the new era.

OB-08

Uphold The LoL's Honour

You represent The Legend of Legiona in every action you take on Skyxion. Act with integrity and national pride. Your conduct is a direct reflection of our people.

Official Documents

THELOLGOV-29-03-2026-0001
The Citizenship Act
Excerpt
Article I — Establishment of Citizenship Section 1.1 — The Legend of Legiona hereby establishes a formal citizenship framework applicable to all players within the sovereign territory of The LoL on the Skyxion server. Section 1.2 — Citizenship of The LoL is non-permanent in nature and shall be bound to the active server era. No citizenship shall persist beyond a server reset without a formal renewal being submitted and approved. Section 1.3 — The Office of the President holds the primary and overriding authority over the granting, renewal, and revocation of all citizenships.
Article II — Eligibility Section 2.1 — Any active player on the Skyxion server is eligible to apply for citizenship in The LoL, subject to the conditions set forth in this Act and any supplementary proclamations issued by the Office of the President. Section 2.2 — The applicant must be at least sixteen (16) years of age at the time of application. Applications from individuals below this age threshold shall be automatically rejected. Section 2.3 — The applicant must not hold any active bans, suspensions, or unresolved administrative violations on the Skyxion server at the time of application.

THELOLGOV-29-03-2026-0002
Citizenship Revocation Ordinance
Excerpt
Article I — Authority of Revocation Section 1.1 — The Office of the President of The Legend of Legiona reserves the unconditional right to revoke the citizenship of any individual at any time and from any location, without prior notice, and without the requirement for stated justification. Section 1.2 — Grounds for revocation include, but are not limited to: violation of Skyxion server rules, acts of hostility or terrorism against The LoL or its citizens, conduct deemed unbecoming of a citizen, or any act found to threaten the stability, reputation, or integrity of The Legend of Legiona. Section 1.3 — A citizen whose citizenship has been revoked may appeal in writing to the Office of the President. The decision of the President on such an appeal is final and binding.
