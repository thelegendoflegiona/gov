Application Status

Enter your reference number to check the current status of your citizenship application. Reference numbers were issued upon successful submission — format: THELOL-CTZN-YYYY-####

Live registry — status updates reflect instantly

Reference numbers are case-insensitive. Example: THELOL-CTZN-2026-4821 — results are bookmarkable via URL.

How Status Checking Works
01 //

Submit Your Application

After submitting via the application form, you receive a reference number like THELOL-CTZN-2026-####. Save it — you need it to check status.

02 //

Government Reviews

The Office of the President reviews all submissions. This may take time depending on the volume of applications received during the current era.

03 //

Check Here / Bookmark

Once a decision has been made, your status appears here instantly — no refresh needed. The URL updates with your ref so you can bookmark and return directly.

04 //

Your The LoL ID

When approved, your The LoL National ID (THELOL-YYYY-#####) will appear here — your singular key to all government services including the TL$ Digital Wallet.
